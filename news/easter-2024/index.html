Easter 2024 Event

TheTime_Guardian - April 2nd, 2024

Happy Easter, all!


I hope you got to experience my April Fools joke yesterday. Esther is over, and Easter has begun!


The event will run through April 2nd through the 7th. The main attractions include:


- Easter Egg decorating! Like in the previous event (2020, oh my), players can decorate an egg for a chance to win a Bunny Pet!


- A skin contest! Submit a skin to (any) staff member, and we will vote for the best. The winner will receive two rune coupons!


- A staff hunt! Hunt for staff NPCs and have a chance to get their special items once every hour.



You can access the Easter event by using /warp easter. Good luck, and have fun!



- TheTime_Guardian, administrator/event coordinator
