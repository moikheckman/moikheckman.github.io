Halloween 2023 - Sky Eden

Golem_Leader - October 27th, 2023

A Sky Eden classic is back: Trick or Treating!


Like the years before, find staff around /warp halloween to get tricks, treats, and maybe a few new items.


You can also participate in the Skeleton Dungeon, which was last seen in 2019 (to be fair, all Sky Eden events were last seen in 2019). Fight single-player to navigate your way out of the dungeon, and maybe earn some loot along the way.


New this year is a brand new, Sky Eden first: A Sky Eden ARG titled 'The Haunting of Hathaway Manor'! Join Laurel and Danny as they search for their friend Sadie and uncover the mysteries of the Hathaway Manor in the Halloween town. This ARG is exclusively limited to the website, in-game Minecraft, and YouTube. You will not need to make any accounts on any websites.


The Halloween event will last through November 5th, but the ARG and the Halloween world will be available until November 19th. Anyone that manages to solve the ARG will be awarded in-game. Collaboration is expected, especially in the dedicated Discord chat (#hathaway-manor-arg), but not required. The person who contributes the most will also likely receive an award.


Happy hunting and Happy Halloween, Edenites!
