An Antihero Interview

November 23th, 2022

On top of being shortlisted for a Watty, Luna on the Run landed me a spot as Badass Author of the Month over at @antiheroesgalore on Wattpad!


In the spotlight was Luna, the anti-heroine of the story, and the process of creating the world she interacts with. I went in-depth on my thoughts while forming her relationships and internal monologue, as well as what it means to be an anti-hero versus a villain.
