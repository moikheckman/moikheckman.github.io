Halloween 2025 - Sky Eden

Golem_Leader - October 28th, 2025

A Sky Eden classic is back: The Halloween event!


Like the years before, find staff around /warp halloween to get tricks, treats, and maybe a few new items. This will also include three clowns with pink names.


There are new quests this year as well, scattered around the carnival! You can find out about the quests by getting an Admissions Ticket from the Ticket Clown. Quest NPCs have a golden name.


The main quest, Carnies Clash!, will feature a competition between two warring factions of clowns, and will have you picking a side. Each side has an item with a special item with an Event-Only enchantment that you are working towards! Only the winning team will have the item available.


Also happening this year is a skin contest! The theme of the contest is just anything Halloween-related (costume, creature, whatever). Submissions begin today. To submit your skin, take a screenshot at the Halloween Skin Contest Tent (picture enclosed) and send it in the Showcase channel in the Sky Eden Discord with the tag "#Halloween2025"! The first-place winner will receive a VIP Rank-Up Coupon, VIP Rune Coupon, ¢5,000, a Halloween-themed chest, and 32 prize tickets! Second place will receive ¢2,000, a VIP Rune Coupon, a Halloween bundle, and 16 prize tickets, and third place will receive ¢1,000, 8 prize tickets, and a special Halloween item! Any other participants will receive 4 prize tickets and ¢500. Submissions will be allowed until November 3rd, and judging will happen promptly after.


Additionally, a Mob Switchup Coupon will be active for the full duration of the event, allowing some scarier mobs to spawn in the overworld.


The Halloween event will last through November 10th, and after that, the winning clown team will have a representative on the Globetrotter, where they will be giving the reward item to anyone who participated in the Carnies Clash! event and earned points for a clown faction.


As always, there are traders that you can interact with. You can use Prize Tickets, obtained from multiple sources, to trade with these NPCs. Trade NPCs will have a "¢" next to their name.


Happy hunting and Happy Halloween, Edenites! And a big thanks to Western_Wolf, Linkylicious, Dovisary, GRENDELYNZ, and Dustified (and myself) for the work put in to renovating the Halloween world! We've finally gotten rid of the ugly black walls around the place!



Golem_Leader, owner
