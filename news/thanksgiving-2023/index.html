Thanksgiving 2023 - Sky Eden

Golem_Leader - November 27th, 2023

Happy Thanksgiving, Sky Eden!


In years past, small little events have been done for this admittedly unimportant holiday in the grand scheme of things.


This year, we've decided to kick it up a notch.


There is a brand new area for this event: /warp thanksgiving, which can be found in the spawn town (Bracketburgh). Here, you can enter the factory where workers are getting the Thanksgiving meals ready.


There's just one problem: they are obviously running behind, and need our help! As an incentive, they have decided to reward us with a special new bow for the food items that are donated the most.


Introducing the Bowsplosion and Bowtion.


Donating bread or pumpkin pie goes towards earning access to the Bowtion, and cookies or golden apples earns access to the Bowsplosion. This event will end on December 4th, so there's a limited time to decide on which bow wins! The winning bow will receive a crafting recipe, and the loser will vanish to the annals of time.
