Esther 2024 Event

TheTime_Guardian - April 1st, 2024

Happy Esther, all!


It's that time of year for the annual Sky Eden Esther event! The event will run through April 1st through the 7th. The main attractions include:


- Esther Egg decorating! Like in the previous event (2020, oh my), players can decorate an egg for a chance to win a Bunny Pet!


- A skin contest! Submit a skin to (any) staff member, and we will vote for the best. The winner will receive two rune coupons!


- A staff hunt! Hunt for staff NPCs and have a chance to get their special items once every hour.



You can access the Esther event by using /warp easter or finding the egg under the Travel Warps section of the Sky Eden Book. Good luck, and have fun!



- TheTime_Guardian, administrator/event coordinator
