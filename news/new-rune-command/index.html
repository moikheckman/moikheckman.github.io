New Rune Command

Golem_Leader - April 20th, 2024

Hey, all!


Phase two of the new coupon system is here, with the big change we've been hoping to bring: adding runes to your items by yourself!


If you have one of the new rune coupons (the light or dark red paper), you can do /rune when holding an item to apply runes.


You can find out more at https://skyeden.org/information/runes. Happy enchanting!
