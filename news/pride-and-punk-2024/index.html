The Pride and 'Punk 2024 Donation Event

Golem_Leader - June 7th, 2024

Happy Pride Month, all!


We will once again be hosting the Pride and 'Punk event this year, with the same donation goal. Functionally, the goals will remain the same, but with new items.


For Pride and 'Punk 2024, any donations received between now and the end of July (July 31st) will be sent to The Trevor Project and will count towards a donation goal. There will be three goal markers: $200, $400, and $500.



$200 Goal: At the $200 goal, everyone will have double vanilla XP, double McMMO xp, and the ability to fly for a week. Additionally, anyone who donated (even if it was one dollar), will receive a rune coupon.


$400 Goal: At the $400 goal, everyone will once again have double vanilla XP, double McMMO xp, and the ability to fly for a week. Additionally, anyone who donated at least ten dollars will receive a special Pride and 'Punk gift box.


$500 Goal: At the $500 goal, everyone will once again have double vanilla XP, double McMMO xp, and the ability to fly for a week. Additionally, EVERYONE will receive the Pride and 'Punk 2023 general gift box, and we'll have a drop party.


After that, every extra $100 will add another week of the perks listed above. Additionally, the highest donator will receive three more VIP Rank-up coupons, to be used/traded as pleased, and a special Pride and 'Punk 2024 Top Donator gift box.


Would you rather donate to a different group, or opt to not have the money donated? I will respect those wishes, no questions asked.


As mentioned above, this event will go until July 31st. You can go to https://www.skyeden.org/donations/ if you choose to donate or would like to view donation progress.
