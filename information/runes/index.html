Runes

Golem_Leader - April 20th, 2024

Occasionally, either from enchanting or from getting coupons, you have the ability to get runes. Runes behave like enchantments, but often have much more interesting effects.



To rune an item with a coupon, hold the item in your hand, and type "/rune". This will opop up a menu of available runes for that item.



Note: Up to ten runes can be applied to one item. A donor rune coupon will be used after all normal rune coupons are gone, unless you are trying to apply Ethereal.



A list of runes can be found at runes.skyeden.org.
