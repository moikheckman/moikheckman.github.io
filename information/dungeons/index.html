Dungeons

Golem_Leader - June 1st, 2023

What's all this ruckus about an end dungeon or hive? Why is Golem_Leader so obsessed with bees?


Dungeons.


Dungeons are a challenging, staff-made, themed event/area where you can fight mobs, solve puzzles, beat bosses, and win prizes, including coupons and special items. Some are multiplayer, some are only available at certain times of the year, and some are bee-themed. Before bees even made it into the game. Golem likes to think he was ahead of the curve on that one. Delusional.


List of dungeons and how to get to them:

The Hive - Found in a random spot on the survival map, marked on the livemap (or at /warp hive)

The End Dungeon - Found wherever the eye of ender takes you (or at /warp enddungeon)

The Mine - A Halloween-exclusive dungeon found in the Halloween world (/warp halloween)
