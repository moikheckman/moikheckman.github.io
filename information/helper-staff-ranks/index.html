Helper and Staff Ranks

Golem_Leader - June 1st, 2023

Helper - Staff's first line of defense. Not technically staff, but can rank you up to forester. Also has direct access to important information about the server; feel free to ask!


Helper+ - Helper's older brother. They can rank you up all the way through the celestial ranks! Also, they can fly. So there's that too.


Moderator - The power of the sun... almost in the palm of my hand. As a general rule, don't ask staff to WorldEdit things. Partially because not all moderators can in the first place.


Moderator+ - Can in fact WorldEdit things. Still don't ask.


Admin - Has sunglasses.
