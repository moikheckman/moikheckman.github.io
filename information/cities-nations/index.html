Cities/Nations

Golem_Leader - June 1st, 2023

Cities and nations are a way to get players together towards a common goal, as well as a fun way to meet new groups of people. Let's take a look at the requirements!


City Requirements:

- 3 active Sky Pilots (including yourself)

- A brewery/bar with the brewery plugin setup

- A Nether Portal

- An Inn with rooms for people

- Crop farms (Wheat, Carrot, Potato, Beet, Nether Wart, Sugar Cane, Melon, Pumpkin, Bamboo, Cocoa Beans)

- Animal farms (Cow, Sheep, Pig, Chicken, Mooshroom)

- A Town Hall with a bulletin board

- A blacksmith with an Iron Block anvil and a Minecraft Anvil, Blast Furnace and normal Furnace

- A Hospital with a regeneration beacon

- A library with a full enchanting table

- 5,000 Wings (The Currency)


Cities get a marker on the livemap, a recognized banner, and a town brew. They can also make themselves a Mob Arena.



Nation Requirements:

- 3 Towns

- Diplomacy Building

- Cathedral

- 10,000 Wings

- National Border Marker (Small border pillars with the national banner)


Diplomacy building and Cathedral don't have to be in a town but it's probably a good idea.

Nations get a marker at their Diplomacy Building, a border on the livemap, the ability to declare a National Treasure, a group chat/rank in the Discord channel, and are able to participate in Nation Battle Royales.



Nation Battle Royales:

A Team deathmatch between two nations, set up between the diplomacy buildings, and approved by staff. The winning nation receives a National Treasure.



National Treasure:

Have a resource you really like? Declare it a national treasure! National treasures will be given in bulk every time your nation wins an event.
